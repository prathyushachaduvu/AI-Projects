Refund Policy

Customers can cancel an order before it has shipped and receive a full refund. If an order has already shipped, the customer must request a return after delivery.

Returns are accepted within 30 days of delivery when the item is unused and in its original packaging. Refunds are processed back to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days after inspection.

Shipping fees are not refundable unless the order was damaged, defective, or shipped incorrectly.
